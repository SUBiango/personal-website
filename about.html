About Me!

A little about the journey.

I have been messing around the web for over two years now. I have mainly focused on WordPress to build websites. I always start my projects from scratch because I love simple, usable and clean websites. I use Balsamiq to sketch up ideas. I will then move over to Adobe XD for design before I jump into WordPress for development.

But a couple of months ago, I decided to take a deep dive under the wild wide west, the web. I took a structured course on HTML and CSS in Coursera to learn responsive web design. I am now learning JavaScript and Bootstrap to enhance my web development skills.

This portfolio is my little corner on the web where I will be pouring myself out and the stuff that I will be building over time.

You can look around to see what I have done, and of course, you can say 'hello' to me here.
