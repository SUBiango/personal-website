Umaru S. Biango

I became fascinated with technology, especially software, at a very young age. In 2015, I taught myself HTML and used it to write my resume.

Seeing the results of my coding in a web browser was like magic to me. Unfortunately, due to certain circumstances, I had to put my exploration of user interface on hold for some time.

A few years ago, I decided to revisit my passion for creating websites. I built a portfolio and began making websites for local organizations in Sierra Leone and Australia.

Earlier this year, I gained a renewed interest in delving deeper into web development by studying JavaScript, React, Sass, Bootstrap, and Redux.

I am well on my way to mastering the MERN stack.
